# Ghost Hunter — Training Guide

This guide walks you through the built-in training mode and prepares you for real investigations.

---

## Starting Training

From the main menu, click **TRAINING**. If you haven't completed training yet, the button pulses gold to draw your attention. You can replay training at any time.

Training takes place in a controlled **Training Facility** — a small two-room environment with no real consequences. Your sanity won't drain, there's no timer, and ghosts won't hunt you.

---

## Training Walkthrough

### Step 1 — Welcome

A dialog box introduces you to the Paranormal Investigation Unit. Click **CONTINUE** to proceed.

### Step 2 — Movement

Learn to move your character using **WASD** or **Arrow Keys**. The training asks you to walk from Room A to Room B (highlighted with a pulsing green border on the right side of the map). Once you enter Room B, the step completes automatically.

### Step 3 — Equipment Selection

Your equipment is displayed in the bottom-right of the HUD. Press **[1]** to select your EMF Reader. The equipment panel pulses to guide your attention. Each owned tool is mapped to a number key (1-4).

### Step 4 — Using Equipment

A training ghost (a Shade) appears in Room B. Walk close to it and press **[E]** to scan with your EMF Reader. In training, detection rates are boosted to 85%, so you'll find evidence quickly. Keep pressing **[E]** if your first reading is inconclusive.

When evidence is found, you'll see a notification: *"Evidence found: EMF Reading!"*

### Step 5 — Switching Tools

Press **[2]** to switch to your Spirit Box, then press **[E]** to scan again. This teaches you that different equipment detects different evidence types. You'll need to cross-reference multiple evidence types to identify a ghost.

### Step 6 — The Journal

Press **[J]** to open your Journal. The Journal shows:
- **Evidence Found** — A checklist of all evidence types, with found evidence highlighted
- **Ghost Identification** — A list of all ghost types you can click to attempt capture

The Journal is your primary tool for solving investigations.

### Step 7 — Ghost Identification

With the Journal open, review your collected evidence. The training ghost is a **Shade**, which leaves EMF and Thermal evidence. Click the ghost name that matches your evidence to attempt capture.

In training, wrong guesses just prompt you to try again — no health penalty. In real investigations, a wrong guess costs 30 HP and ends the mission.

### Step 8 — Sanity & Hunts

A dialog explains the sanity system:
- **Sanity drains** passively during investigations, faster when near ghosts
- When sanity drops below a ghost's **hunt threshold**, it may initiate a **Hunt**
- During a Hunt, the ghost turns aggressive, moves fast, and chases you
- Hunts last 8-15 seconds — keep moving to survive
- The **Resilience** skill reduces sanity drain

### Step 9 — Skills & Progression

A dialog explains the upgrade systems:
- **XP** levels you up, unlocking harder locations
- **Currency** buys skill upgrades and new equipment
- Four skills improve different aspects of your capabilities
- Equipment gets better the more you use it (proficiency system)

### Step 10 — Complete

You're done! Click **FINISH** to return to the main menu. Your save now records that training has been completed.

## After Training — Your First Real Hunt

### Recommended First Steps

1. **Start at the Abandoned House** — It's Tier 1 with a single low-danger ghost
2. **Use both starter tools** — Switch between EMF Reader and Spirit Box
3. **Open your Journal early** — Start narrowing down ghost types as soon as you find evidence
4. **Don't rush the capture** — Gather at least 2 evidence types before guessing
5. **Watch the timer** — You have 3 minutes per investigation

### Understanding Evidence

Each ghost leaves exactly 2-3 types of evidence. Your job is to figure out which combination matches:

```
Shade:       EMF + Thermal
Whisper:     Spirit Box + Orbs
Poltergeist: EMF + Spirit Box + UV
Wraith:      Thermal + UV + Orbs
Banshee:     EMF + Thermal + Spirit Box
```

**Process of elimination works.** If you find EMF and Spirit Box evidence but no Thermal, it's likely a Poltergeist (you'd need UV to confirm).

### Equipment You Don't Have Yet

You start with the EMF Reader and Spirit Box. Two more tools are available from the **EQUIPMENT** shop:

- **Thermal Camera ($200)** — Detects cold spots. Needed for Shade, Wraith, and Banshee
- **UV Light ($350)** — Reveals ectoplasmic traces. Needed for Poltergeist and Wraith

Save up from your first few hunts to buy the Thermal Camera. Without it, you can only fully identify Whispers (Spirit Box + Orbs are passively detected).

### When Things Go Wrong

- **Health drops to 0** — Investigation fails. You keep partial XP from evidence gathered
- **Timer runs out** — Investigation fails. Same partial XP
- **Wrong identification** — You lose 30 HP and the investigation ends
- **Correct ID but capture fails** — 60% base success rate. The Capture skill increases this

You can heal at the Equipment shop for $50.

---

## Skill Upgrade Priority

For new players, recommended upgrade order:

1. **Detection 1-2** — More equipment range means safer scanning from farther away
2. **Identification 1-2** — Evidence appears faster, reducing time spent near ghosts
3. **Resilience 1-3** — Reduces sanity drain, giving you more time before hunts start
4. **Capture 1-2** — Increases capture success from 60% to 72%

After that, balance upgrades based on what's causing you trouble.

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Ghost Orbs are passive.** You don't need equipment to detect them — just walk near a ghost that produces orbs and they'll appear on their own
- **Equipment proficiency matters.** The more you use a specific tool, the better it gets (increased range and detection chance, up to +30%)
- **Sanity thresholds vary.** A Shade only hunts below 20% sanity, but a Banshee hunts at 50%. Higher-tier ghosts are more aggressive
- **During hunts, the ghost targets you directly.** Its speed is 80x its base speed multiplier. Banshees are the fastest hunters
- **The Bestiary tracks your progress.** Once you've seen a ghost, its evidence requirements are recorded for reference
